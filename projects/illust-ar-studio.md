## Project Overview

Illust Space is an open augmented reality (AR) platform that empowers digital creators to publish and share their art globally. The platform combines AR technology with blockchain to create immersive experiences that can be discovered anywhere in the world through mobile browsers. As CTO, Sabin Hertz led the technical transformation that took the company from startup to successful seed funding round.
