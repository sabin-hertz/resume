## Project Overview

Particle Ink is a groundbreaking multi-platform experience that merges the physical and digital worlds through cutting-edge Augmented Reality (AR), Artificial Intelligence (AI), and blockchain technology. The platform creates an immersive ecosystem known as the "2.5th Dimension" that spans gaming, live entertainment, digital collectibles, and phygital activations. Users can discover and interact with this fantastical universe through their mobile devices, blending their real-world surroundings with digital adventures.
